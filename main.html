MUMBAI-PUNE-MUMBAI

short summary

The story revolves around two young characters: a boy named Gautam Pradhan (Swapnil Joshi) from Pune and a girl named Gauri Deshpande (Mukta Barve) from Mumbai.
The film begins with Gauri arriving in Pune in order to meet a prospective groom who is mentioned as "Hridaymardyam" after the name of his residential colony.
Gautam is busy playing cricket with his friends and she inadvertently comes across him to ask him for Hridaymardyam's address.
With the help of the directions given by Gautam, Gauri reaches Hridaymardyam's house only to find the door locked.
